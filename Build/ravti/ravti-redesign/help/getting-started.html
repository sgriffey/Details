The Job Flow

The dashboard is broken-up into four buckets, which we use to signify the four phases a job must pass through prior to completion. As a job progresses it will move from bucket to bucket.

Jobs start as RFQ's. Once propsoals are submitted (and won), the job will move into the scheduling bucket. Once scheduled, the job will carry out into performing where it will remain until we have documented proof that the job was indeed performed. Once performed we will release a purchase order and wait to be invoiced. Once the invoice is received, the job will be removed from the dashboard card counts, moving to the completed jobs tab.

To-do's

The dashboard buckets update in real-time. When a new job enters that bucket's phase, the count on badge will update, reflecting the quantity of jobs (in this case 11) which require action.

Completing To-do's

The majority of your Ravti experience should be simply sharing documents, from your system to ours. During any job phase which requires additional files: a dedicated uploader is visible, paired alongside a table showing jobs needing documents uploaded.

Job Type Identification

We give each job a category badge to help quick identifications. Our system buckets jobs into four categories: Service Calls, Repairs, Replacements, and Preventative Maintenance programs.
