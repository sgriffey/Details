Personas

It all starts with empathy. It's important to consistently think about our users and their context as we build their tooling. “It’s our responsibility to be less clever, to make choices that service a wide spectrum of people, to think of ourselves as gracious hosts who never assume our guests know our home as well as we do.”

Facility Engineer

Also Reffered To As:

Building Engineer

UX Goals:

  • Be Helpful
  • Be Responsive (in Feedback)
  • Be Assistive
  • Be Handy
  • Be Factual

Behaviors/Traits:

  • Initiates work requests
  • View equipment (& history by facility)
  • Receives Notifications (Need to change filters, perform inspection, etc)
  • Orders Parts
  • Reviews scope RFQ's and pricing
  • Likes to be collaborative with Field Tech's
  • Desk typically in a dark corner in buried part of building (think janitor's office)
  • Walkie Talkie
  • Has both Preferred Vendors (Invites them as well) &
  • One of few mobile viewers. Needs to do everything on fly.

Influencers:

  • Pressures from Property Manager and hierarchy
  • Speed (e.g. Do they have feedback on *when* a vendor will be on-site?)
  • Feedback loops, being informed (urgency could cause work outside Ravti)
  • Mechanical contractor perks (baseball games, trips to the keys, etc.)

Property Manager

Also Reffered To As:

P.M. , Manager

UX Goals:

  • Be an organizer
  • Be resourceful
  • Be insightful
  • Be holistic
  • Be obvious

Behaviors/Traits:

  • Makes final call on which proposal to go with
  • Biggest pain in a** of her day: (getting more than 1 price)
  • She wants to see high level: how much $ we spending on HVAC (all of building budget)
  • Leasing (new tenant wants to know life expectancy on equipment, what the condition and diagnostics for new space is going to look like: give new onboardings a exported set )
  • Leasing compliance
  • Initiate maintenance programs
  • Wants high level summaries of services being done (has to be kept in loop)
  • FYI: 80/20 Female to Male role ratio

Influencers:

  • Budget (Typically agreed on w/ management for Quaterly / Annual Forecasting)
  • Time. Think busy and impatient
  • Org hierarchy. Has upper management to please
  • Doesn't enjoy not being a domain expert. Wants to "speak the language"

Field Technician

Also Reffered To As:

Mechanical, Mechancial Contractor, Tech, Vendor

UX Goals:

  • Be obvious
  • Be easy
  • Be un-intrusive
  • Be lightweight
  • Be effortless
  • Be convenient
  • Be frictionless

Behaviors/Traits:

  • We're cutting into his pie
  • Has processes already has to abide with, we're a thorn
  • Dirt under the fingernails type person
  • Dishevled, does work, passes tickets/reports to teamember back @ office
  • On the fly

Influencers:

  • Weather (Summer Heat + Rooftops)
  • Time (Everytime come out, automatic trip-charge plus hourly rate
  • A limited resource, service call comes up, will leave preventative maintenance
  • Day to day wear and tear (greasy hands, cracked mobile screen, etc.)
  • Stubborness to old ways
