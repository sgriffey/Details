Memo

This little micro-site reveals our guidelines. The hope is for this site to grow with us overtime — establishing a home for our brand's design process along-the-way. Unifying our team's outlook on the attitudes and behavior we build products for is key. As designer's we're constantly making assumptions about what our audience knows, and with time this conviction becomes more clearly engrained.

Team members (both new and old) should use this as a reference; familiarizing themselves with our audience, and the patterns with in which we communicate.
