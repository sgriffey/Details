Glossary

Industry vernacular that is thrown around both internally and externally.

Condition Inspection
A one-off analysis measuring the health of said HVAC equipment
Condition Score
Proprietary algorithm measuring life expectancy. 0-100 scale.
Invoice
What our owners/tenants receive. Indicates types, quantities, and agreed prices for products or services.
Lead Time
estimated time between order placement and shipment / arrival.
Preferred Vendors
A facility’s set of favored mechanical contractors (Typically the existing maintenance provider is at the top of this list.)
Preventative Maintenance (P.M.)
An annual program of routine HVAC checkups and diagnostics. Typically done quarterly, P.M. programs help ensure equipment is operating at maximum efficiency, helping extend equipment lifespans.
Proposal
The collective offer from the vendors to the building owners detailing the cost of goods and labor. (Information includes: job summary, scopes, equipment/warranty information, prices, freight, etc.)
Purchase Order
What our vendors receive. Indicates types, quantities, and agreed prices for products or services.
Request For Quote (RFQ)
Submitted to one or more potential vendors (mechanical contractors, manufacturers, parts suppliers) eliciting quotations for a pre-defined job. Us asking vendors for bids on what it would cost for their goods/labor according to the job summary/scope at hand.
Scope
A description of the work to be performed.
Service Call
A facility / site visit made by a field technician. Usually spurred by a facility engineer after equipment malfunction has been recognized and un-diagnosable.
Service Ticket
The receipt / invoice Field Technician’s fill out post-Service Call / facility visit. Typically done with triplicate paper (3-layer carbon copy).
Trip Charge
The guaranteed, baseline fee that mechanical contractor’s charge for sending a technician out for a site visit.
Vendor
Could refer to either mechanical contractors, manufacturers, or parts suppliers.
Work Request
General Term for a submission by owners (or tenants) requesting Ravti services.
